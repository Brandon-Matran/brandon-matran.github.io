About Me

In my previous life I was a critical care RN, providing care to the sickest of patients. My interest in tech began when I was exposed to Epic, a healthcare charting system for caregivers. I volunteered to be a SuperUser for EPIC so that I could get some experience using the charting system and I was intrigued by it all, the data flow, the components, how data was used in a meaningful way for a user. , I decided to delve into the world of programming by enrolling through Hack Reactor. Now, through my journey, I have learned to make apps using Python and Javascript. I want to continue this field because I love the feeling of creating something meaningful for others.
